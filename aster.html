What are the types of Bone Marrow Transplant?


  • Autologus Bone Marrow Transplant - Marrow cells are collected from the patient's own body and transfused back after chemothrapy.
  • Allogenic Bone Marrow Transplant(Matched Sibling donor)- Replacement of dieseased or defective marrow with marrow from a genetically (HLA) matched sibiling donor.
  • Allogenic Bone Marrow Transplant(Matched Sibling donor)- Replacement of dieseased or defective marrow with marrow from a genetically (HLA) matched donor obtained from bone marrow registries in india and abroad.
  • Haplo-identical bone marrow - Replacement of dieseased and defective marrow with marrow from the parent
  • Umbilical cord Bone Marrow Transplant - Replacement of dieseased and defective marrow with marrow from a genetically (HLA) matched umbilical cord donor obtained from umbilical cord registries in india and abroad.
